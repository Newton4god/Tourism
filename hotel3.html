Atican Beach Resort-Lagos State

This Beach Resort -one of the many private beach resorts in Lagos- is located in Lekki, Lagos. It is a truly exceptional ocean-side destination. Moreover, it is a good location for fun lovers or a family. Indeed it is a perfect getaway trip. It has that touch of nature with modern facilities. You are ensured of having a truly glorious vacation. Meanwhile, there is so much to do at this resort. You can engage in water games or volleyball. There is also a barbecue grill, a nightclub and an Asun spot. This location boasts a restaurant with several mouth-watering local and intercontinental cuisines. The accommodation here is spectacular. The rooms are comfortable and adequately furnished. You get daily newspaper delivery at this resort. In short, this is truly a resort to visit.

Inagbe Grand Resort & Leisure-Lagos State

The Inagbe Grand Resort & Leisure is located off the Lagos coast along Snake Island. It is a modern beach resort that has a mix of culture, nature and a fabulous view. However, the resort can be accessed by a boat ride from the Regatta jetty. Nonetheless, this is sure to be a thrilling ride for adventure lovers.

The resort provides a peaceful and lovely environment for its numerous visitors. It is indeed an ideal holiday spot for anyone.Inagbe has lots of activities like spa treatments, water sports, table tennis and beach soccer. In addition to a fully equipped gym, horse riding, a jungle tour, etc. The resort has various accommodations that come in different sizes. You can choose from a single room to 6 bedrooms. The rooms are tastefully furnished. Also, they have a top-notch restaurant. You will love this home away from home.
