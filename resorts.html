Thus, here are the top best resorts in Nigeria. In this articele, we have coupled 10 of the most incredible Resorts:


La Campagne Tropicana-Lagos State

This Beach Resort is located along the Lekki/Epe expressway. It is believed to be one of the most visited beach resorts in Africa. Of course, this is due to its natural, serene and paradise-like environment. The resort consists of the Atlantic Ocean, a savannah, a freshwater lake, and a large sandy beach. This resort offers numerous fun activities. They include hiking, hunting, horse riding, water sports, beach volleyball & football, etc. The resort also provides luxurious accommodation, spa treatments and massage therapy. In addition to a fully equipped gym and a 27-hole golf course. Moreover, the restaurant at La Campagne Tropicana is amazing. It is guaranteed to satisfy your dining needs to the fullest.

Obudu Mountain Resort-Cross River State

This Mountain Resort is located in southern Nigeria. Conversely, it is a short distance from Cameroon -one of Nigeria's neighbouring countries. The resort is also famous for having Africa's longest cable ride. Subsequently, the line runs from the foot of the mountain to the top of the resort. This destination in Calabar is truly a place with stunning views and a serene environment. Also, it will be a thrill for adventure seekers and a family getaway. The resort features a water park, a fully equipped gym, forest hikes and a golf course. In addition to nght-time bonfires, tours, canopy walkways and horseback riding. The accommodations here are ultra-modern. But it still keeps to the natural uniqueness that surrounds it. Therefore, this is a great place to relax and have an adventure.

La Manga Luxury Beach Villas-Lagos State

This Luxury Beach Villa is located on the island of Ilashe in Lagos state. Consequently, it is near the Atlantic Ocean. And this gives it that calm and serene atmosphere. This is an exciting place to stay and leave all of your stress behind. It is magical and appeals to tourists from far and wide. The accommodation features Spanish architecturally built villas. There are four elegantly furnished bedrooms in addition to sliding doors that open to private balconies. Each villa consists of a jacuzzi, a living room, a massage room and a bar. Plus a private lounge, a fully equipped kitchen and a swimming pool. Hence, this is a prime vacation resort to relax at.

Le Meridien Ibom Icon Hotel & Golf Resort- Akwa Ibom State

This resort is located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. Subsequently, it covers over 135 hectares of land. This is the perfect vacation spot for lovers of golf, nature and serenity. The resort is also situated in a rich palm forest. This is guaranteed to give you a refreshing, memorable, and beautiful vacation. Besides, the golf course here is easily the biggest in Nigeria. It also boasts of a unique 18 holes golf course over hills. The resort has various outdoor sports. These include lawn tennis, squash, biking, swimming, etc. Accommodations are also provided at this lavish resort. Plus each room comes fully furnished, with beautiful views.

White Deck Beach Resort-Lagos State

This Beach Resort is located on Lagos Island. It is a truly magnificent getaway spot for a blissful holiday. The resort is an exotic blend of culture, nature and modern charm. The environment is also tranquil and had lots of fun. It is perfect for the family or that special person. There are various outdoor and indoor activities to take part in. You will want to participate in the numerous water sports available. Above all, this beach resort is the epitome of luxury and comfort. It has various spacious room options and features to make your stay memorable. The decor of the accommodation matches the elegant theme of the resort. You will love what this resort has to offer.
