Adults-Only Resorts

Sometimes you need a getaway and a family vacation just won't cut it. That's when the beauty of an adults-only resort comes in handy. These luxurious, child-free resorts are the perfect setting for the ultimate romantic escape. In this Article we've coupled up some of the best Adults-only resorts suitable for your taste.

Post Ranch Inn in Big Sur

Post Ranch Inn is 100-acres of paradise in Big Sur, California. Visitors can take advantage of the morning yoga and guided nature walks during their stay. The resort has 40 unique and innovative rooms and suites for guests (there's even a treehouse you can stay in) with sweeping views of the Pacific coast. The renowned restaurant, Sierra Mar, and award-winning spa sweeten the deal. Post Ranch Inn only welcomes guests over the age of 18. You will love what this resort has to offer.

Spa Village Resort

Spa Village Resort Tembok is a romantic sanctuary where tranquillity and wellbeing is paramount to the experience. Not only does the resort offer healing retreats and therapeutic spa treatments for guests, but it encourages everyone to unplug from everyday life by discouraging the use of phones in public areas. Coupled with the delicious dining options and luxury accommodations, the Spa Village Resort Tembok is the ultimate escape. The resort welcomes guests age 16 and above.

Auberge du Soleil

The romantic Auberge du Soleil in Napa Valley offers an intimate adults-only experience in the heart of California's wine country. From the 50 luxury guest rooms and suites to the breathtaking views of California's mountains and vineyards, this 33-acre property is the ultimate escape. The Michelin-starred Restaurant at Auberge du Soleil offers an unforgettable dining experience, along with an award-winning 15,000 bottle wine cellar. Guests can reflect and relax at the Auberge du Soleil Spa, with nurturing treatments and luxury services set in a stunning landscape.

Hurawalhi Island

Island Resort is an adults-only paradise. The resort features gorgeous villas suspended over the Indian Ocean and incredible dining options, like a Champagne pavilion and 5.8 Undersea Restaurant, the world's largest all-glass undersea restaurant where you can have a truly one-of-a-kind dining experience. The resort's luxury spa, unique excursions, and activities available make the Maldives your playground.
